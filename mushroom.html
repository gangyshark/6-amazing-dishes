MUSHROOM RISOTTO

Ingredients

  • 6 cups of chicken broth, or 6 cups of vegitable broth
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 lbs of portobello mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 lbs white mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 shallots diced
  • 1 1/2 cup of arborio rice
  • 1/2 cup dry wine
  • 1/4 tablespoon salt and pepper
  • 3 tablespoons finely chopped chives
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 1/3 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese

How to make it

  1. In a sauce pan, heat the broth on low heat
  2. Take a large pot and add 2 tablespoons of olive oil on medium heat. Put the mushrooms in the pan and cook until soft. remove mushrooms and their liquid and put to the side.
  3. Add 1 tablespoon olive oil to skillet, and stir in the shallots. After a minute, add the rice, and coat it with oil. After two minutes or the rice takes on a pale, golden color, pour in the wine and stir until the wine is absorbed.
  4. Once absorbed, add 1/2 cup of the broth to the rice, and stir until it's absorbed. Continue to do this until all the broth is added.
  5. remove from the heat and stir in the mushrooms, along with their liquid, butter, chives, and parmesan. Season with the salt and pepper.
  6. You could add other things such as shrimp, chicken, or other vegitables
  7. Enjoy!

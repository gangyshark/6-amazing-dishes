LOBSTER TAIL

Ingredients

  • 2 Lobster tail
  • 1/2 cup of butter, melted
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground paprika
  • 1/2 garlic salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon Lemon pepper
  • 1 teaspoon parsley, minced
  • 1/2 a lemon

How to make it

  1. Preheat oven to broil
  2. Place Lobster tails on a flat sheet tray that is oven safe. Cut the tails down the the center of its back until you reach the edge of the tail (dont cut the whole thing, you're only opening the top of the shell).
  3. Pull the meat and the shell apart, but make sure to leave the meat in the shell, and leave the bottom of the tail in tact with the shell.
  4. Take your butter, paprika, garlic salt, lemon pepper, and parsely, and mix them together. Then take a brush and dip it into the seasoned butter and brush the Lobster meat.
  5. Broil the Lobster tail until the meat is lightly browned and lobster meat is obaque. This takes about 5 to 10 minutes
  6. Enjoy!

GRILLED TROUT

Ingredients

  • Fresh trout, preferebly a boat load
  • Lemons

How to make it

Before you start:

  1. Preheat charcoal to glowing and placed at one edge of the grill
  2. Cut lemon into slices about 1/8 inch thick
  3. Pat clean fresh trout dry
  4. Place 2-3 lemon slices into trout main cavity

Cooking the trout:

  1. Sear trout on one side directly over glowing hot coals until dark brown (about 4-6 minutes)
  2. Flip trout over and sear over glowing hot coals (3-5 minutes)
  3. Spin the grill 180 degrees placing the trout away form the coals
  4. Add addition trout over the hot coals
  5. Put lid on grill
  6. Remove trout from indirect heat after flipping next batch
  7. Enjoy!

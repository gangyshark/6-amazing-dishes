BUFFALO CHICKEN DIP

Ingredients

  • 4 large boneless chicken breasts
  • 16oz of ranch
  • 14oz Franks Red Hot
  • 16oz cream cheese
  • 8oz of shredded chedder
  • 8oz shredded monterey

How to make it

  1. Boil the 4 chicken breasts in a large pot until completely cooked through.
  2. Take the chicken out of the pot and lay on a cutting board and let cool
  3. Turn the crock pot onto high.
  4. Shred the chicken and once shredded put in the crock pot.
  5. Once the chicken is shredded, put all the other ingredients in the crock pot.
  6. Put the lid on the crock pot and let sit for 90 to 120 minutes.
  7. Take an egg beater or mixer and mix the buffalo chicken dip.
  8. Turn the crock pot heat down to warm
  9. Enjoy!

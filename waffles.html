Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 3/4 cups of milk
  • 1/2 cup vegitable oil or butter(melted)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

How to make it

  1. Preheat waffle maker to high
  2. In a mixing bowl stir together flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In another mixing bowl mix together while stirring at the same time, eggs (already whipped together), milk, oil, and vanilla.
  4. Slowly mix the second mixing bowl with the first. You'll know that its properly mized when the batter is moist and kind of lumpy.
  5. Take a brush and lightly brush the waffle maker with olive oil or melted butter. This will help keep the waffles from sticking. Read the manufacturer's instructions on how long to cook waffles, and how much you can put in the maker. They should feel kind of hard and crunchy, but not tough or hard as a rock.
  6. remove with either a fork or tongs, and then put on a plate.
  7. repeat process until done with the batter
  8. Enjoy!
